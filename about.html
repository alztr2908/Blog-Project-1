My name is John and living the simple life saved my life.

I used to be a pack rat. I had so many things that I though that I loved, but the truth was I couldn't even appreciate any of it becasue there was too much.

Things were terrible. I had a job that I hated, a home that I didn't want to live in, and I couldn't hold a relationship.

How I turned things around

I was on the verge of a breakdown when I got rid of everything. I leterally put 90% of my possessions in the garbage, sold my place and moved into a smaller apartmen, and I quit my job.

It wasn't easy at all, but everything around me was in such a bad placce, I didn't know what else to do. And it worked.

Now I live a simple life

Now that I'm a living a simple life, things are so much better. I;m less stressed, enjoy life and work more, and I have more free time to enjoy

With how much it turned my life around, I felt like I had no choice but to start sharing how I did it, and how others can benefit from living a simple life as well, which is why I started this site!
